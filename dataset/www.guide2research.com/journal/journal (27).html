Aims & Scope of the Journal

ACM Transactions on Programming Languages and Systems publishes original research articles in the areas of General Computer Science and Software Engineering & Programming. The journal is targeted at professors, practitioners and researchers who are interested in such topics of scientific research . The publishing policy for ACM Transactions on Programming Languages and Systems is to publish new original contributions that have been extensively reviewed by competent academic peers. The journal welcomes submissions from the research community where emphasis will be placed on the innovativeness and the practical impact of the published work.

ACM Transactions on Programming Languages and Systems is listed in a wide scope of abstracting and indexing databases such as Scopus, Web of Science and Guide2Research. A number of leading scholars have published their research contributions at this Journal including Krishnendu Chatterjee, Daniel Kroening, Nobuko Yoshida and Joost-Pieter Katoen.

For further information on the instructions and submission provisions for authors, you are advised to see the journal website for ACM Transactions on Programming Languages and Systems at https://dl.acm.org/journal/toplas .
