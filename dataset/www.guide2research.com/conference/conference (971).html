Conference Call for Papers

It’s our great pleasure to invite you to join us for the 2020 2nd International Conference on Robotics, Intelligent Control and Artificial Intelligence (RICAI 2020)[2020年第二届机器人、智能控制与人工智能国际学术会议], which will be held on October 17-19, 2020 in Shanghai, China. RICAI 2020 will provide a forum within the international academic and engineering community in the field of Robotics, Intelligent Control and Artificial Intelligence.

Website：http://ic-ricai.org

E-mail：ICRICAI@126.com

*Accepted and presented papers will be published in ACM-International Conference Proceedings Series (ISBN: 978-1-4503-8830-6), which will be archived in the ACM Digital Library, and indexed by Ei Compendex, Scopus, etc.

【Call For Papers】

Topics of interest for submission include, but are not limited to:

Robot structure design and control

Intelligent perception systems

Multi-mode dynamic perception and natural interactions

multi-robot intelligent aggregation mechanisms and operation platforms

Swarm intelligence in multi-robot systems and system architecture

Robot navigation, positioning and autonomous control

Robot perception and data fusion

Bio-machine-electric system and fusion

Unmanned system control technology

Human-machine-environment natural interaction and interaction

Robot kinematics and dynamics

Multi-robot collaborative control

Robot grabbing and operation

Robot behavior decision and control

Robot motion and path planning

Robot operating system

Intelligent control theory

Intelligent control system

Fuzzy logic control

Neural network control

Hierarchical control

Expert system

Genetic algorithm

Adaptive control technology

Self-organizing control technology

Self-learning control technology

Neural networks and applications

Intelligent information system

Biometric identification

Affective computing

Data mining

Fuzzy logic and soft computing

Intelligent manufacturing

Pilotless automobile

Machine learning

Unmanned aerial vehicle

Natural language processing……

We are looking forward to meeting you in the very beautiful city Shanghai, China, during October 17-19, 2020. Definitely, RICAI 2020 will provide you a pleasant experience, new contacts and happy stay in Shanghai.
