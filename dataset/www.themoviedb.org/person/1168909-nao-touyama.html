Nao Touyama

Biography

Touyama Nao was born March 11, 1992 in Tokyo, Japan. She is a freelance voice actress (seiyuu) formerly affiliated with Arts Vision. Touyama made her professional debut in 2010 as the voice of Nakagawa Kanon in the television anime series Kami nomi zo Shiru Sekai.
