Takaya Hashi

Biography

Takaya Hashi (土師 孝也 Hashi Takaya, born September 8, 1952) is a veteran Japanese voice actor and actor affiliated with the Seinenza Theater Company. His real name is Takaya Katō (加藤 孝也 Katō Takaya).
