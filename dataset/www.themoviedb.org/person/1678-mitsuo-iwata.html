Mitsuo Iwata

Biography

Mitsuo Iwata was born on July 31, 1967 in Saitama, Japan. He is an actor and voice actor, known for Akira (1988), Paprika (2006) and D.Gray-man (2006). He is married to Rikako Aikawa.
