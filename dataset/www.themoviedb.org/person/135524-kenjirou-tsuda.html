Kenjirou Tsuda

Biography

Kenjirō Tsuda is a Japanese actor and voice actor. He is affiliated at Stardust Promotion as an actor and Mediarte Entertainment Works as a voice actor. Before he joined Stardust Promotion he was part of Tristone Entertainment.
